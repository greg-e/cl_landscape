# Microsoft 365 Licensing Recommendation

> Prices as of April 2026 (annual commitment, per user/month billed monthly).  
> Verify current pricing at [microsoft.com/microsoft-365/business/compare-all-plans](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/business/compare-all-plans).

---

## Recommended Plan: Microsoft 365 Business Standard

**$12.50 / user / month** (annual commitment)  
**$15.00 / user / month** (month-to-month, no annual commitment)

This is the right tier for CL Landscape's office and admin users. It includes everything needed for the full operations stack described in this project.

### What's Included

| Capability | Relevance to CL Landscape |
|---|---|
| Outlook (desktop app) | Required for QuickBooks Desktop invoice sending |
| Exchange Online (business email) | Reliable invoice delivery from your domain |
| SharePoint Online | Document library for estimates, quotes, contracts |
| Microsoft Teams | Daily coordination, weekly renewal reviews |
| Microsoft Lists | Contract pipeline tracking |
| Power Automate | Renewal reminders (90/60/30-day workflows) |
| OneDrive (1 TB/user) | File backup and access from the field |

| Bookings | Optional: customer scheduling |

## All Business Plan Options

| Plan | Price/User/Month | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Microsoft 365 Business Basic** | $6.00 | Email + web apps only — no desktop Outlook or Office |
| **Microsoft 365 Apps for Business** | $8.25 | Desktop Office apps + OneDrive — no Exchange email |
| **Microsoft 365 Business Standard** ✅ | $12.50 | Full stack: email, desktop apps, Teams, SharePoint, Lists, Power Automate |
| **Microsoft 365 Business Premium** | $22.00 | Standard + advanced security (Intune, Defender, Azure AD P1) |

> **Why not Business Basic ($6)?** QuickBooks Desktop requires the full Outlook desktop application to send invoices through your business mailbox. Business Basic only includes web-based Outlook (OWA), which is not compatible with QuickBooks Desktop's email integration.

> **Why not Business Premium ($22)?** The additional cost is justified when you have multiple employees with managed devices, compliance requirements, or a heightened security need. For a 1–3 person operation, Business Standard is sufficient.

## Field Staff Option (Optional)

If crew members or field staff need Teams access for scheduling and communication only, Microsoft 365 Frontline Worker plans are available at a lower cost:

| Plan | Price/User/Month | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Microsoft 365 F1** | $2.25 | Teams, SharePoint (read), OneDrive (2 GB), web apps only |
| **Microsoft 365 F3** | $8.00 | Teams, Exchange, Office web apps, OneDrive (2 GB) |

> Frontline plans are limited to users without a fixed workstation and cannot be mixed on the same account as full Business plans without proper licensing separation.

## Add-Ons to Evaluate

| Add-On | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Microsoft Defender for Business (standalone) | $3.00/user/month | Endpoint protection if not on Premium |
| Azure AD P1 (Entra ID P1) | $6.00/user/month | Conditional Access, MFA policies — included in Premium |
| Power Automate per-user plan | $15.00/user/month | Only needed if exceeding standard connector limits in flows |

For CL Landscape's current workflow automation (renewal reminders, pipeline updates), the Power Automate capabilities included in Business Standard are sufficient. No add-on needed.

## Purchasing Notes

- **Annual commitment** locks the per-user price and is ~17% cheaper than month-to-month.
- Purchase directly from [admin.microsoft.com](https://admin.microsoft.com) or through a Microsoft Partner/CSP for potential volume discounts.
- Adding users later is straightforward — licenses are per-user with no seat minimums.
- You can mix Business Standard and Frontline F1 in the same tenant.
