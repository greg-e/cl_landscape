# CL Landscape — Business Priorities

This document outlines the five core priorities for improving and growing the business.

---

## 1. Reliable Invoice Delivery
Ensure every invoice reaches the customer consistently and on time. This includes selecting a dependable delivery method (email, mail, or portal), tracking delivery confirmation, and establishing a follow-up process for unpaid or unacknowledged invoices.

---

## 2. Local Computer Network and Backup Storage Solutions
Set up a reliable local network and a backup storage strategy to protect business data. This covers hardware selection, network configuration, automated backup schedules, and offsite or cloud redundancy to prevent data loss.

---

## 3. Accommodating Customer Payment Preferences
Explore and implement multiple payment options so customers can pay the way they prefer. This may include check, ACH/bank transfer, credit card, and digital payment platforms — with a goal of reducing friction and improving on-time collections.

---

## 4. A One-Page Professional Contract and Renewal Strategy
Develop a clear, concise one-page service contract that protects both parties. Pair it with a proactive strategy for renewals and rate increases, including timing, communication approach, and documentation.

---

## 5. Cost Tracking — Understanding Profit Price
Build a system to track all costs associated with each job or account (labor, materials, fuel, equipment, overhead) so the true cost is known and a profitable price can be set and maintained with confidence.
